# Hotel Entrance Background Image

## Required Image
Place your hotel entrance image as: `Hotel_entrance.png` in the `/public` folder

## Image Specifications
- **Filename**: Hotel_entrance.png
- **Location**: /public/Hotel_entrance.png
- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Content**: Hotel lobby/entrance with reception desk, guests, entrance doors

## Suggested Image Content
- Reception desk visible
- Hotel lobby view
- Guests walking/standing
- Entrance doors
- Professional hotel atmosphere
- Good lighting

## Temporary Fallback
If you don't have an image yet, the kiosk will show a dark gradient background until the image is added.

## How to Add
1. Save your hotel entrance image as `Hotel_entrance.png`
2. Place it in: `c:\ACE\mcw-hotel\mcw-hotelmdu-nokia-master\mcw-hotelmdu-nokia-master\public\`
3. Refresh the application

The image will automatically be used as the full-screen background for the Guest Information kiosk tab.
